La Ventana Big Animals Ocean Safari
Mobulas, dolphins, orcas and whales expedtion

This amazing tour is focused on interacting and swimming with dolphins, mobulas, orcas and with different species of big marine animals.  

A location far away from the busy streets of Cabo San Lucas and experience Baja like it should be. This site is located on the fabled East Cape, a 2 hour drive from Cabo San Lucas. There we will find calm waters surrounded by the beautiful island of Cerralvo. The channel between La Ventana and Cerralvo serves as a highway for a variety of ocean life. We have regular sightings of Bottlenose Dolphins, Turtles, Mobula Rays, Sea Lions, Humpback Whales and more. This also happens to be the best location for observing and interacting with Orcas.  An opportunity not to be missed! 

Our passion for the ocean is apparent and we want to share that with all of our guests. We also want to instill a sense of respect and admiration for all the incredible animals we encounter. We are fortunate to have such diversity accessible to us and our guests and this is what makes Baja and our excursion so special.

La Ventana Big Animals Ocean Safari 

This adventure begins with a pick up at your Cabo San Lucas hotel (Note: For our guests in San Jose del Cabo, we meet at a convenient meeting location). After pick up, enjoy a 2 hour drive that meanders along the pacific coast before crossing through the hills and arriving to the Sea of Cortez. While we start our tour, we are always scanning the horizon in search of any interesting wildlife and our experienced guide and captains may have a few special locations they like to check as well. Once we find something interesting we evaluate their behavior and if everything is calm we jump in. Our excursions spend as much time in the water as possible.

Nature is unpredictable and we cannot guarantee any specific interactions, however, our high level of experience and the incredible abundance of marine life give us a very high chance to spot and swim with different species on the same tour. For example, we see dolphins pretty much on every tour. Our goal is to provide the best up close and personal underwater encounters with the most beautiful and iconic species of Baja California Sur.


Light snacks, water, and box lunch will be provided on your safari. You are always welcome to bring anything extra onboard to enjoy.

Tour Duration: 5am pick up- 5pm drop off.

Included: mask fins snorkel- please note wetsuits are available for rent of $10
