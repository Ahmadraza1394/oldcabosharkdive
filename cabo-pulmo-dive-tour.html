Cabo Pulmo Dive Tour

Cabo Pulmo is the world best marine park, the biomass increase of 463% in the last few years! Bull sharks, huge school of fishes, amazing corals, incredible biodiversity. All dive sites are 10 minute boat ride from the beach, average depths are 50-75 feet (15 to 25 meters) and there are sites suitable for all levels of certified divers. Visibility varies a lot during the whole season, ranging from 20 feet in winter, to 100 feet in the summer / fall. The water temperature is 65 - 75 feet (Jan-May) and 75 - 88feet (Dec-Jan). Typically, we use 5-7 mm wetsuit in the winter and shorties 3mm in the summer/fall season.
