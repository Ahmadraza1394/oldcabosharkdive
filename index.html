Mexico's #1 Shark Diving Company since 2015

CABO SHARK DIVE is a FIVE-STAR shark diving company, the first and only official and certified shark diving operator in Los Cabos and Baja California Sur with regular license and permit of the Port Authorities. We have been successfully and safely operating in Cabo since 2015, we provide the best and safest shark diving, snorkeling, free diving and shark swimming experience in Cabo San Lucas with great customer service and thousands of happy customers every year!

All our tours are suitable for everyone and don't require diving certification. All our trips are available both as regular tours and as full private charters, we also organise and lead tailor - made expeditions.

Our amazing Team consists of Marine Biologists, professional underwater photographers, PADI certified diving instructors and dive masters, professional sharks handlers and sharks behavioural experts with more than 16 YEARS of experience working in the shark diving industry with premiere sharks species worldwide. You are in very good hands!

EDUCATE, INSPIRE AND ENJOYING SWIMMING AND DIVING WITH SHARKS AND WITH THE MOST ICONIC OCEAN WILDLIFE IN BAJA
