SAFETY ON TOUR

The Cabo Shark Snorkeling Tour is ABSOLUTELY SAFE, we have 100% safety record with no accidents in all our tour history. Cabo Shark Dive has the Official Authorisation and Permit of the Mexican Federal Institutions and Port Authorities for shark diving/snorkelling activities in Cabo San Lucas. Our Shark diving/snorkelling tour follow the rigorous professional global protocols of the shark diving industry, with tested and reliable safety measures. With more than 15 years of experience in shark diving operations we guarantee that you are in the best hands! The safety and comfort of our guest is a priority for us, in and out of the water. All our divers and watchers will receive a careful and direct supervision during all the experience by our certified and experienced guides. Only when one of our staff have deemed it safe to enter the water with the sharks will our guests be able to jump in! We are also fully equipped with satellite phones, radio and gps, full Medical kit and all the safety devices required by the Port Authorities. Our crew members are trained as EMERGENCY FIRST RESPONDERS and EFR Instructors and they are qualified to use all medical equipment onboard and handle any kind of situation. Our experienced and professional shark diving guides are always the first entering the water and the last to exit. With a constant and direct supervision of the snorkelers, they will ensure safety, comfort and enjoyment during the whole experience. Anything you need in and out of the water, just ask our guides, they are always ready to help!

THE MOST EXCITING TOUR IN CABO

This is a real Eco Tour, you really help sharks and marine conservation while diving with us!

The CABO SHARK SNORKELING TOUR is our most popular tour and the best boat tour in Cabo San Lucas. On this trip you will snorkel and free dive with sharks out of the cage under the professional guidance and supervision of our trusted shark guides and marine biologists. No diving certification required NOT ONLY SHARKS: we specifically target sharks, but often we can see and swim with giant mantas, dolphins, orcas, whales, sea turtles, marlin, sea lions and mahi mahi. Our objective is getting in the water safely with all marine life. Our awesome shark dive and snorkeling tour occur a few miles offshore, in the blue waters of the Sea of Cortez and Pacific Ocean. Most of our exclusive dive sites are located only 25 minutes boat ride from the Marina, safe and comfortable! Before leaving the dock we will provide snorkelling gear to all our guests, to make sure that everyone has the right equipment with the right size! Once on the spot, our certified shark guide will give a detailed briefing about safety rules and procedures, proper use of the snorkelling gear and will explain how to interact with our sharks in the best and safest way! Once in the water, we have all the time we want to swim with sharks, of course the duration of the experience depends on the sharks behaviour and on how long they want to stay around, and usually they stay for a very long time!

 If you don't want to get in the water, a very good option is the SHARK WATCHING TOUR, where you can enjoy the thrill and excitement of seeing sharks up close and learning about them from the deck of our comfortable boat. The SHARK WATCHING TOUR is designed for families and groups of people that want to go out and see sharks in the wild without getting wet or having just a nice boat ride. Shark Watching Tour is essentially the same as the Shark Snorkeling tour, the only difference being our guests simply watch the sharks from the boat. To make our Shark Watching Tour more fun for our guests, we often bring the sharks right up to the surface next to the boat, this allows our shark viewers to see these animals up close and personal and take great pictures.

WHAT MAKES THIS ACTIVITY UNIQUE?

Fun, adrenaline, education and conservation. The Cabo Shark Snorkeling Tour is absolutely the best activity you can do in Cabo San Lucas, and in general the best experience you can have in the ocean. It’s safe, extremely fun and educational! It’s difficult to describe the mix of emotions, the thrill and the happiness you will feel while swimming safely with sharks in their natural environment, you have to try it! During the tour, you will learn interesting and important facts about shark biology and ecology that will help you to understand better how these wonderful creatures behave. Our marine biologists and sharks behavioral experts will tell you everything you want to know about sharks, how we are deeply involved in shark conservation and how you can help us to protect them. Sharks have more than 480 MILLIONS YEARS OF EVOLUTION, they are real Prehistoric Animals and they are the most beautiful and majestic marine creatures. Being in the water with them, having a real and natural interaction  is a priceless privilege.

Shark Species

Blue shark (Prionace glauca)
length 6-9 ft

The most elegant, cute and playful shark, like a big puppy. They stay for hours around the boat, they swim slow and they come peacefully close to everyone!

Blues shark peak season: From January until May with multiple sightings on every tours

Mako shark (Isurus oxyrinchus)
length 4-12 ft

The fastest shark in the world, and the most exciting to dive with! Pure adrenaline! The best shark experience you can have in the ocean, without the cage!

Mako shark peak season: December good, Top months: From February until July with multiple sightings on every tours. On the peak season we have an average of 3+ mako on the same dive every dive. We saw up to 9 makos at the same time in the water!

Silky shark (Carcharhinus falciformis) length 5-8ft

Curious and cool! They come really close and they live in huge schools, you can swim with lots of them at the same time!

Silky Sharks peak season: From May until December, big silky sharks aggregations (50+) July with multiple sighting on every tours

Smooth hammerhead Shark (Sphyrna zygaena) length 6-14 ft

Mysterious, elusive and unique, a prehistoric animal! A rare shark species, a powerful interaction with our biggest shark, the dream of any underwater photographers!

Smooth Hammer head peak season: February good, Top months From March until July with sightings on every tours
