We constantly monitor the official weather forecast and we communicate with our local Port Authorities to make sure conditions will be safe for the activity. WE CANNOT GUARANTEE WEATHER CONDITIONS OR TRAVEL INTERRUPTIONS, THE ACTIVITY TAKE PLACE IN THE PACIFIC OCEAN SO THE WEATHER AND THE MARINE CONDITIONS CAN CHANGE ANYTIME.  Since the weather is still out of our control, sometimes activities may be partially canceled and/or modified to better fit the local weather conditions. Once in Magdalena bay, we don't provide refund in case the activities is modified or partially canceled due to bad weather conditions.  We operate on a flexible schedule and will do our best to make sure you are able to get the full experience of the trip.
