Dive Cabo San Lucas

Cabo San Lucas has some of the BEST Scuba diving in the world. All dive sites are 10 minute boat ride from the beach, average depths are 50-75 feet (15 to 25 meters) and there are sites suitable for all levels of certified divers.Inside the protection of the Bay the conditions are great for diving with rarely currents and typically good visibility. Perfect if you dive for the first time! Huge cliffs and deep canyons, big school of fishes, pelagic animals, mobulas, sea horses, healthy coral formations, tropical fishes and playful sea lions. Live an unforgettable experience diving in the sea of Cortez, the aquarium of the world. Visibility varies a lot during the whole season, from 40 to 100 feet. The water temperature is 65-75 feet (Jan-May) and 75-88 feet (Jun-Dec). Typically, we use 5-7mm wetsuit in the winter and shorties 3mm i n the summer/fall season.
